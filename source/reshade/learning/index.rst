
Learning
========

This section offers a comprehensive guide to various learning resources, covering Shaders, Computer Graphics, Mathematics, and Programming. It provides structured learning paths and in-depth materials for both beginners and advanced users.

Key topics include:

- **Writing Shaders**: Dive into the fundamentals of shader programming with ReShade FX. This extensive guide covers everything from basic structure and key concepts to step-by-step shader creation and advanced techniques.
- **Resources**: Explore a curated list of learning materials, tools, and platforms, categorized by source type, with a special emphasis on beginner-friendly options.
- **Literature**: Access a collection of open-access scholarly sources, including archives, journal directories, and peer-reviewed journals, for in-depth research in computer science, mathematics, and computer graphics.
